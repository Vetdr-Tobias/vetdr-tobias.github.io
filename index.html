DATA ANALYSIS OF MEXICANS RESTAURANTS RATINGS WITH POWER BI

Do you want to have a restaurant in Mexico and probably you’re not armed with meaningful info about the industry in Mexico? What about cuisines? SALES REPORT USING MICROSOFT EXCEL DATA VISUALIZATION DASHBOARD

Visualizing they say, is better. This is a visual display of key insights derived from a consolidated sales data set of a group of products in Canada. The Key insights are · *Total Revenue · *Total Quantity sold · *Average Revenue · *No of manufacturers · *Sales trend · *Top 10 Products by Revenue · *Top 3 States by Revenue · *Trends by Quantity (Unit) Sold · *Revenue Contribution by category ***The visual display revealed two major patterns in the sales data set, which are 1. An increase in quantity (units) of the product sold resulted in an increase in sales in corresponding months under review and vice versa. 2. The products are not for people living in the rural environment as the Rural category contributed just 9% to the revenue against 87% from those of the Urban category. These may be due to differences in income earnings or education (product enlightenment). Thus the data-driven decision/action that can be taken here is that organization should focus more on the Urban areas especially in the months of March and April while educating the rural populace about their products.
